The quickest & easiest plugin to add social media share buttons to your WordPress posts. Facebook, Twitter, Reddit, WhatsApp and more.

== Description ==

WordPress Social Sharing Icons is the quickest way to add social media icons/buttons to your website. 

It lets you easily setup and add social sharing buttons on your posts and pages with options to add to the top, bottom or both top & bottom of your content.

The plugin supports all popular networks:
Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Pinterest, Linkedin, Google+, YouTube, Pinterest, VKontakte, StumbleUpon, Reddit, Whatsapp, Buffer and more

Let your visitors share your content and reach a much larger audience.

= No coding required! =

Features

*  Choose from 10+ of the most popular social media services
*  Font Awesome integration for beautiful icons
*  Official brand colours for instantly recongnisable social media services
*  Customize layout options with choices of inserting at the bottom, top or top & bottom of your posts/pages
*  Quick and easy setup

== Installation ==

1. Install 'WordPress Social Sharing Icons' either via the WordPress.org plugin repository or by uploading the 'social-share-buttons' folder to your /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Use the Settings -> WordPress Social Sharing Icons screen to configure & enable the plugin.
